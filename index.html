House of Hope Foundation for Kids with Cancer Inc.

The House of Hope is a Hospital-based school catering to children of school age who are patients of the SPMC Children's Cancer Institute. Families come together, and occasionally they fall apart from the strain of dealing with a catastrophic illness, specifically cancer. Finding cures and continuously providing care for these little ones is a tremendous challenge. Despite the difficulties and lack of resources, there is HOPE that we have in abundance.

I am supporting this charity organization because it improves childhood cancer survival rates and finds kinder, more effective treatments to reduce long-term side effects. It aims to provide psychosocial support for parents and families with children afflicted with cancer. It also brings hope and better understanding of child cancer through education and to empower parents and families in their fight against cancer.
